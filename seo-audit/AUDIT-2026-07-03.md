## What's missing — prioritized

### Critical
1. **Blog is empty (0 posts in sitemap).** The 120-article plan + FAQ schema infra exist on
   the `dev` branch but aren't deployed, and no posts are loaded in Sanity. This is the only
   channel that can carry E-E-A-T and citation-worthy content. → Deploy dev, create the
   author + categories in Sanity, load the 8 Day-1 hubs.
2. **Zero named authorship anywhere.** No name/photo/bio on /about ("small independent team"),
   no bylines. Biggest single E-E-A-T blocker for both Google and AI engines. → Name a real
   person on /about, create the Sanity author, byline every post.
3. **NEW FINDING — /receipt-help/* is a 219-page swapped-noun cluster** (71 brands × 3 intents;
   diffed pages are 503 words with only the brand name changed). Largest duplicate cluster on
   the site, same scaled-content risk class as the brand pages. → Inject 1–2 unique verifiable
   facts per brand (receipt lookup path specifics, app names, retention windows — the data is
   genuinely different per brand) or consolidate/noindex the weakest intent variant.

### High
4. **No off-site authority signals at all** — no sameAs profiles, zero Wikipedia/Reddit/
   ProductHunt/YouTube footprint. AI engines cite brands they can corroborate. → Off-site work:
   LinkedIn company page, X profile, ProductHunt launch, one YouTube demo video (YouTube
   mentions correlate strongest with AI citations). Then add `sameAs` array to Organization
   JSON-LD (code, 5 min).
5. **FAQ answers too short for AI citation** — currently 19–64 words; the citation sweet spot
   is ~130–165. → Expand the 2–3 highest-value answers per key page while keeping the first
   sentence as the direct answer.
6. **Junk Sanity document live**: /blog/page-82a781a83af13f51 renders an empty post. → Delete
   in Sanity Studio.

### Medium
7. **llms-full.txt missing** (404). → Generate a fuller markdown file inlining FAQ content,
   template list and pricing so LLMs ingest without crawling. (code, low effort)
8. **/examples index still ~590 KB HTML** (target ≤300 KB; was 1 MB). → Cut to 12 previews per
   page or lazy-render below-the-fold cards.
9. **No dates on templated pages' schema** — add dateModified where data actually changes.
10. **Verify IndexNow actually pings on publish** (endpoint is admin-gated, unverifiable
    externally).

### Low
11. rsl.xml AI-licensing file (emerging standard, low effort).
12. Reframe a few homepage H2s as questions ("What do you need to make a receipt?").
13. Connect GSC/CrUX (seo-google skill) to replace lab estimates with field data and watch
    indexation of the 900-URL templated set — decide pruning based on real coverage data.
